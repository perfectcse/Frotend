# 🎯 Interview Questions

## What is localStorage?

localStorage stores data inside the browser even after refreshing the page.

---

## Why use localStorage?

To preserve the shopping cart after refreshing or reopening the browser.

---

## What is State Management?

State Management is the process of keeping application data synchronized with the UI.

---

## Why use reduce()?

reduce() is used to calculate the total amount of all products in the cart.

---

## Difference between filter() and find()?

find() returns the first matching object.

filter() returns all matching objects.

---

## What is DOM Manipulation?

Changing HTML elements dynamically using JavaScript.
